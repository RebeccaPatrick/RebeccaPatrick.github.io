Seven

Seven is the main character from a Chinese comedy series called ‘Scissor Seven’. This is one of the most casual cosplays I have collated.

Wig: I trimmed a long side parted wig, teasing and hair spraying it into a gravity-defying cartoonish spikey ponytail.

Scissors: I hand-cut the unique shape using EVA foam with an exacto knife and then sanded it smooth. I created a mechanism so they can open and close like real scissors, by sanding down a chopstick, creating a decretive clay stop featuring the characters' logo and contact cementing it through the foam.

Hoodie: I sketched and cut out a stencil to guide fabric paint into cleaner lines.

Adjustments: To ensure the shorts were the correct length I bought a pair of joggers and hemmed them to the desired length.

Overall, I am happy with this build. Next time I mainly would like to improve the scissors, in particular the paint job. I want to learn how to create a more realistic metallic finish. Potentially commanding that technique with 3d modeling and printing to make the shape more robust. Although the fabric paint applied well to the hoodie and has lasted, if I made this again, I would apply an applique technique that would leave a sharper and leaner finish.
